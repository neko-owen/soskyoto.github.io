Kyoto Green Map

Places in Kyoto that promote sustainability

The basic principles of this map are Reduce, Reuse, Repair, Recycle. We’ve got a wealth of second-hand stores, recycle stations, low plastic grocery options, and restaurants that cater to plant-based diets right here in Kyoto!
Many of us want to make a difference when it comes to sustainability, and one way we have the power to make a change is with the decision of where to spend our money. This map aims to make it easier to find places in Kyoto that help move us toward a greener, more circular economy, where waste is no longer part of the equation.
